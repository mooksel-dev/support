# Privacy Policy

**Last updated: November 16, 2024**

## Introduction

This privacy policy applies to the Disciplinio app (hereby referred to as "Application") for mobile and desktop devices that was created by mookdel.dev (hereby referred to as "Service Provider") as a Commercial service.

## Information Collection and Use

The Application does not collect, transmit, or share any personal information. All data created and managed within the Application is stored locally on your device.

### Data Storage

- All tasks, settings, and user data are stored locally on your device using SwiftData
- No data is transmitted to external servers
- No analytics or tracking tools are implemented
- No personal information is collected

## Third Party Access

The Application does not share any information with third parties as no data is collected or transmitted from your device.

## Your Rights

Since all data is stored locally on your device:

- **Data Access**: You have complete access to all your data through the Application
- **Data Deletion**: You can delete your data at any time through the Application's settings or by uninstalling the Application
- **Data Portability**: Your data remains on your device and is not stored in any external system

## Children's Privacy

The Application does not collect any information from anyone, including children under the age of 13.

## Security

Since all data is stored locally on your device and the Application does not collect or transmit any information, the security of your data depends on the security measures of your device (device passcode, encryption, etc.).

## Changes to This Privacy Policy

The Service Provider may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. You are advised to review this page periodically for any changes. Changes to this Privacy Policy are effective when they are posted on this page.

This privacy policy is effective as of November 16, 2024.
